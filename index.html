Frequently Asked Questions

How do online yoga classes work with Dr. Seema?
Classes are conducted live over secure video sessions. Unlike standard recordings, Dr. Seema provides real-time postural corrections, breath alignment coaching, and active camera guidance to ensure you perform every asana safely and correctly.
I am an absolute beginner; are these classes suitable?
Yes, absolutely. Dr. Seema's teaching methodology is tailored to welcome beginners. Rhythms and postures are introduced progressively, starting with simple joint release techniques (Sukshma Vyayama) before moving to advanced flows.
Can I consult Dr. Seema for specific medical conditions?
Yes, she combines Ayurvedic diagnostics with tailored yoga therapy. She specializes in guiding practitioners who have back stiffness, chronic neck pain, stress-related disorders, digestive issues, and metabolic imbalances.
How do I schedule my free live trial session?
Simply fill out the booking form below with your details and preferred morning slot. Our team will contact you via email or WhatsApp to coordinate your setup and send the secure live class invite link.
