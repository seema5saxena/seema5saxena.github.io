Meet Dr. Seema Saxena

Dr. Seema is an Ayurvedic doctor and certified yoga practitioner with over 25 years of experience in promoting health and well-being. She was among the first doctors at Patanjali Yogpeeth, contributing to its foundation and success as part of Swami Ramdev's core team.

Certified by the Morarji Desai National Institute of Yoga, New Delhi, under the Ministry of Ayush, she combines extensive medical knowledge with yoga expertise to deliver trusted and effective guidance for a healthier lifestyle.
